Cherie

Project Overview

This project focuses on creating a clean, modern website for a local bakery to help customers easily browse products, check daily specials, and place orders. The goal was to bring the bakery’s warmth and identity online through a simple, accessible experience that supports both discovery and convenience

Problem

Cherie needed a simple, modern website that clearly presented their products and made it easier for customers to explore their menu. Their online presence was limited, information was scattered, and customers had no quick way to browse offerings or learn what made the bakery special.

Solution

I designed a simple, modern website that lets customers explore Cherie’s baked goods, place orders easily, and get clear information without confusion. The goal was to give the bakery a warm, trustworthy online presence that matches its local charm.  Now you can browse the project visuals and see how the final design brings Cherie’s identity to life

Learnings

This project helped me practice building a brand-aligned visual system and translating a physical shop’s atmosphere into a digital experience. I also improved at organizing product content, structuring clear navigation, and choosing UI elements that feel both functional and inviting
