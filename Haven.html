Haven

Project Overview

A mental-health platform that combines self-tests, therapy booking, and daily wellness tools in one seamless experience.

Problem

Mental-health resources are fragmented, making it hard for people to know what they need or where to start.

My Approach

To design a clear and supportive mental-health platform, I followed a structured UX process focused on understanding user needs before moving into solutions.

Learnings

This project taught me how important it is to slow down and actually understand what people need before jumping into design. I learned how to turn abstract mental-health challenges into clear problems that can be solved with simple digital tools.  It also helped me recognize the value of structuring information, running small iterations, and staying flexible when ideas shift. Most importantly, I learned how to translate real user struggles into features that feel supportive, not overwhelming.
