The End

Project Overview

A streamlined mobile app designed to simplify browsing movies, checking showtimes, and securing cinema tickets in just a few steps. The goal was to remove friction, speed up the booking flow, and create a smooth experience from movie discovery to seat selection

Problem

Users often struggle with slow, confusing ticket-booking experiences too many steps, unclear seat selection, and difficulty finding showtimes quickly. They need a faster, clearer way to browse movies, pick seats, and confirm their tickets without frustration

Solution

I designed a simple and intuitive ticket-booking flow that removes unnecessary steps and keeps users focused on selecting a movie, choosing a seat, and paying smoothly. The interface highlights clarity, quick actions, and real-time seat updates

Learnings

Designing this app helped me improve user flow clarity, simplifying booking steps, and organizing UI elements for faster task completion. I also practiced highlighting key actions and visual hierarchy to guide users naturally
