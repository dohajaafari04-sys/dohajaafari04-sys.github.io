GO Trade

Project Overview

A simplified trading app designed to help users monitor markets, place trades quickly, and understand their portfolio at a glance. The goal was to reduce complexity, streamline key actions, and create a cleaner, more intuitive trading experience for beginners and active users alike

Problem

Users struggled to make confident trading decisions because existing platforms felt cluttered, overwhelming, and hard to navigate

Solution

I designed a clear and streamlined trading experience that removes friction and helps users act with confidence. The interface simplifies market insights, improves navigation between assets, and makes key actions like buying, selling, and tracking performance more intuitive.  Below are the screens that illustrate how the final solution works in practice

Learnings

Working on this trading app helped me understand how important it is to balance clarity with information density. I learned how to structure complex data like charts, prices, and market movements into layouts that feel readable at a glance.  It also pushed me to be more intentional with UI elements: choosing components that guide the user’s focus, using color to signal meaning, and creating visual hierarchy that reduces cognitive load.  Overall, this project strengthened my ability to design interfaces that stay clean, fast to understand, and supportive during high-pressure decisions
